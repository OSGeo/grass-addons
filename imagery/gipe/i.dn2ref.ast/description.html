DESCRIPTION

i.dn2ref.ast calculates the Top Of Atmosphere (TOA) reflectance for Terra-Aster visible, NIR and SWIR bands (9 layers) from DN values. It is useful after importing your Aster imagery from storage format that is generally in standard DN values range.
