HydroFOSS: a GIS embedded approach for Free & Open Source Hydrological modeling
###############################################################################
HydroFOSS is:
1)distributed – the hydrological variables are continuously described in the space.
2)Physically based – all the involved variables have a physical meaning.
3)Continuous – operates over an extended period of time, determining flow rates and conditions during both runoff periods and periods of no surface runoff.
4)Modular – is a combination of different modules describing the processes involved in the rainfall-runoff process.
5)GIS embedded – is fully developed into a GIS system by using the GIS's commands and library functions.
6)Open Source – developed by using exclusively Free and Open Source Software.
