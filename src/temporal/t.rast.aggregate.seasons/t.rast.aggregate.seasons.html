DESCRIPTION

t.rast.seasons aggregates an input space time raster dataset at astronomical seasons level using a statistical method selected by the user. It uses t.rast.aggregate.ds to detect and copy the astronomical seasons granularity. Astronomical seasons are defined as:
  • Spring 20 March - 21 June
  • Summer 21 June - 20 September
  • Autumn 20 September - 21 December
  • Winter 21 December - 20 March (following year)

EXAMPLES

Calculate seasonal data from 
    t.rast.seasons input=mystrds basename=mystrds_seasons
