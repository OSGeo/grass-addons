DESCRIPTION

t.rast.aggregate.seasons calculate a new space time raster dataset with statics at astronomical seasonal level from an input space time raster dataset. It use t.rast.aggregate.ds to calculate aggragated data. Astronomical seasons are defined as:
  • Spring 20 March - 21 June
  • Summer 21 June - 20 September
  • Autumn 20 September - 21 December
  • Winter 21 December - 20 March (following year)

EXAMPLES

Calculate seasonal data from 
    t.rast.aggregate.seasons input=mystrds basename=mystrds_seasons
