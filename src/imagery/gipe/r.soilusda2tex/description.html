DESCRIPTION

r.soilusda2tex calculates the soil texture fractions using USDA soil classes. It outputs soil texture proportions (sand, silt, clay) in range [0.0-1.0].

NOTES

Soil Classes ------------ 0 clay 1 sandy clay 2 silty clay 3 sandy clay loam 4 clay loam 5 silty clay loam 6 sand 7 loamy sand 8 sandy loam 9 loam 10 silt loam 11 silt
